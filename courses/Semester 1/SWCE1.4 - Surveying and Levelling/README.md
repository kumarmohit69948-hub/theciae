# SWCE1.4 — Surveying and Levelling

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Introduction of Subject
- **Lecture 2:** Classification and basic principles of surveying
- **Lecture 3:** Linear measurements
- **Lecture 4:** Chain surveying
- **Lecture 5:** Cross staff survey
- **Lecture 6:** Compass survey
- **Lecture 7:** Errors in measurements, their elimination and correction
- **Lecture 8:** Plane table surveying
- **Lecture 9:** Different methods of Plane table survey
- **Lecture 10:** Fundament about Levelling
- **Lecture 11:** Levelling difficulties and errors in levelling
- **Lecture 13:** Computation of area and volume
- **Lecture 15:** Introduction to setting out of curves
- **Lecture 16:** Introduction about advanced instruments like Total Station, Electronic
- **Lecture 17:** Introduction to GPS survey
