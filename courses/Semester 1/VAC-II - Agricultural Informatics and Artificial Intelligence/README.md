# VAC-II — Agricultural Informatics and Artificial Intelligence

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Module 1: Introduction to Computers and Basic Applications
- **Lecture 2:** Anatomy of Computers: Hardware, software, input/output devices
- **Lecture 3:** Memory Concepts and Units of Memory
- **Lecture 4:** Operating Systems: Definition, types, functions, and applications
- **Lecture 5:** MS Office Applications (MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint): Document creation, editing, formatting, graphing, and data presentation
- **Lecture 6:** Database Management Systems (DBMS): Introduction, types, and applications in agriculture
- **Lecture 7:** Introduction to the Internet and WWW: Concepts, components, and uses in agriculture
- **Lecture 8:** Computer Programming Basics: Introduction to Visual Basic, Java
- **Lecture 10:** ICT Applications in Agriculture: Role of IT in farming and rural development
- **Lecture 11:** Computer Models in Agriculture: Statistical models, weather analysis, and crop simulation models
- **Lecture 12:** Computer-Controlled Devices: Automated systems for Agri-input management
- **Lecture 13:** IT Applications for Crop Water and Nutrient Requirements
- **Lecture 14:** Smartphone Applications in Agriculture: Market price tracking, farm advice, postharvest management
- **Lecture 15:** Geospatial Technology in Agriculture: Concepts, techniques, and applications
- **Lecture 16:** Decision Support Systems (DSS): Concepts, components, and applications
- **Lecture 17:** Agriculture Expert Systems: Soil Information Systems for farm decision support
- **Lecture 18:** Preparation of Crop Calendars Using IT Tools
- **Lecture 19:** Introduction to Artificial Intelligence: Definition, background, and applications
- **Lecture 20:** Turing Test and Control Strategies
- **Lecture 21:** Search Strategies in AI: Breadth-first search, depth-first search
- **Lecture 22:** Heuristic Search Techniques: Best-first search, A* algorithm
- **Lecture 23:** Big Data and IoT in Agriculture: Use cases and benefits
- **Lecture 24:** AI-Based Autonomous Crop and Livestock Management
- **Lecture 25:** AI in Crop Health Monitoring and Intelligent Pesticide Application
- **Lecture 26:** AI in Precision Agriculture: Yield mapping and predictive analysis
- **Lecture 27:** Automatic Weeding and Harvesting Systems Using AI
- **Lecture 28:** AI in Sorting and Food Processing Applications
- **Lecture 30:** Digital India and Initiatives for Agricultural Digitalization
- **Lecture 31:** India Digital Ecosystem of Agriculture (IDEA) and Future
- **Lecture 32:** Case Studies and Industry Trends in Agricultural Informatics & AI
