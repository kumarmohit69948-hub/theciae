# AS3.2 — Engineering Physics

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Magnetism: Dia, para and ferro-magnetism- classification
- **Lecture 2:** Langrevin's theory of dia, and para magnetism, adiabatic demagnetization
- **Lecture 3:** Weiss molecular field theory
- **Lecture 5:** Time dependent and time independent Schrodinger equation
- **Lecture 6:** Spectroscopy: Qualitative explanation of Zeeman effect, Stark effect and
- **Lecture 9:** Superconductivity; critical magnetic field, Meissner effect, isotope effect
- **Lecture 10:** Josephson's effect, DC and AC squids, introduction to high Tc 1 superconductors
- **Lecture 11:** LASERS and MASERS: Spontaneous and stimulated emission, Einstein A 2 and B coefficients, population inversion
- **Lecture 12:** He, Ne and Ruby lasers
- **Lecture 13:** Ammonia and Ruby masers
- **Lecture 14:** Holography and optical fiber: optical fiber- physical structure, basic theory, 2 type of modes
- **Lecture 16:** Illumination: laws of illumination, luminous flux, luminous intensity, candle 2 power, brightness
