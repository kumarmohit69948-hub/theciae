# FMPE3.4 — Engineering Mechanics

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Basic concepts of engineering mechanics, statics, dynamics, kinetics, scalar 1 quantities, vector quantities, systems of units; Laws of forces
- **Lecture 2:** Composition and resolution of forces, analytical method, graphical method
- **Lecture 4:** Centre of gravity (CG) of simple geometrical figures, CG by moments, plane 2 figures, axis of references
- **Lecture 5:** CG of symmetric sections, unsymmetrical sections, solid bodies and cut sections
- **Lecture 6:** Moment of inertia: methods of finding out M.I., methods of integration, M.I. of 2 different sections
- **Lecture 7:** Theorem of perpendicular axes, parallel axes, M.I. of composite sections and cut 2 sections
- **Lecture 8:** Frictional forces, static friction, dynamic friction, limiting friction, normal 1 reaction, angle of friction, coefficient of friction, laws of friction
- **Lecture 9:** Equilibrium of a body lying in horizontal and inclined planes, ladder friction; 3 wedge friction, screw friction, screw jack
- **Lecture 10:** Analysis of simple framed structures, methods of sections, for citable, methods 3 of joints, hinged joints, roller support, vertical and inclined loads
- **Lecture 11:** Simple stresses and strain, Hooke's law, Poisson's ratio, modulus of elasticity
- **Lecture 12:** Shear force and bending moment, fundamentals of shear force and bending 4 moment, SFD and BMD of cantilever and simply supported and overhanging beams, point of contra-flexure
- **Lecture 13:** Torsion of circular shaft, torsional effect, hoop stress, power transmitted by a 2 shaft
- **Lecture 14:** Principal stresses and strain, analysis of plane and complex stress, principal 3 planes and principal Stresses, Mohr's circle, finding out principal stresses, different analysis
