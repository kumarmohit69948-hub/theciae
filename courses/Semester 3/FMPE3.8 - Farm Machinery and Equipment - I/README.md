# FMPE3.8 — Farm Machinery and Equipment - I

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Introduction to farm mechanization, Classification of farm machines
- **Lecture 2:** Unit operations in crop production, Identification and selection of machines 2 for various operations on the farm
- **Lecture 3:** Introduction to materials used in construction of farm machines, Heat treatment processes and their use in farm machines
- **Lecture 4:** Familiarization with properties of materials used for critical and functional 2 components of agricultural machines, Different types of steels and alloys for 2 agricultural applications, Identification of heat treatment processes 2 especially for the agricultural machinery components
- **Lecture 5:** Introduction to seed-bed preparation and its classification
- **Lecture 6:** Introduction to machines used for primary tillage, secondary tillage, rotary tillage, deep tillage and minimum tillage, viz. mould-board plough, disc plough, chisel plough, sub-soiler, harrows, puddler, cultivators, etc
- **Lecture 7:** Identification to major functional components of tillage machinery, attachments of the machinery with tractor and adjustments during operation
- **Lecture 8:** Introduction to hitching systems and controls of farm machinery
- **Lecture 9:** Familiarization with land reclamation and earth moving equipment
- **Lecture 10:** Calculation of field capacities and field efficiency
- **Lecture 11:** Measurement of draft of tillage machinery and calculations for power requirement for the tillage machines
- **Lecture 12:** Calculation for economics of machinery usage, comparison of ownership with hiring of machines
- **Lecture 13:** Introduction to sowing, planting and transplanting equipment, viz. seed drills, no-till drills, strip-till drills
- **Lecture 14:** Introduction to different types of planters, bed-planters and other planting equipment for crops like sugarcane and potato. Calibration of seed drills/planters
- **Lecture 15:** Study of different types of furrow openers and metering systems in drills and planters, Adjustments during operation
