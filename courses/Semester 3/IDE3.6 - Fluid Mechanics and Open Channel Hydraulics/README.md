# IDE3.6 — Fluid Mechanics and Open Channel Hydraulics

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Properties of fluids: ideal and real fluid, units
- **Lecture 2:** Pressure and its measurement
- **Lecture 3:** Pascal's law, pressure forces on plane and curved surfaces, centre of 1 pressure, pressure diagram
- **Lecture 4:** Application of hydrostatics in engineering structures
- **Lecture 5:** Buoyancy, Archimede's principle, metacentre and metacentric height, 1 condition of floatation and stability of submerged and floating bodies
- **Lecture 6:** Kinematics of fluid flow: Lagrangian and Eulerian description of fluid 2 motion, continuity equation, path lines, streak lines and stream lines, stream 2 function, velocity potential and flow net
- **Lecture 7:** Types of fluid flow, translation, rotation, circulation and vorticity, vortex motion
- **Lecture 8:** Dynamics of fluid flow, Bernoulli's theorem, venturimeter, orifice meter and 2 pitot tube, siphon Flow through orifices (measurement of discharge, 1 measurement of time), flow through mouthpieces; Flow over notches, flow 32 over weirs, end contraction of rectangular weirs, ventilation of weirs, various types of nappe
- **Lecture 10:** Chezy's formula for loss of head in pipes, flow through simple and compound pipes, transmission of power through pipes
- **Lecture 11:** Open channel design and hydraulics: Chezy's formula, Bazin's formula
- **Lecture 12:** Discharge measurement in open channels: current meter
- **Lecture 13:** Dimensional analysis and similitude: Rayleigh's method and Buckingham's
- **Lecture 14:** Introduction to fluid machinery
