## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Introduction to plant protection equipment, Different types of sprayers and 1 dusters
- **Lecture 2:** Classification of sprayers and sprays, Types of nozzles, Calculations for 2 calibration of sprayers and chemical application rates
- **Lecture 3:** Introduction to interculture equipment, Weeders- different types of manual and 2 powered weeders, Functional requirements of weeders and main components
- **Lecture 4:** Introduction to different types of fertilizer application methods and equipment
- **Lecture 5:** Study of harvesting operation of crops, harvesting methods, harvesting 2 terminology
- **Lecture 6:** Study of mowers­ types, constructional details, working and adjustments
- **Lecture 7:** Study of shear type harvesting devices- cutter bar, inertia forces, counter 2 balancing, terminology, cutting pattern
- **Lecture 8:** Study of reapers, binders and windrowers- principle of operation and 2 constructional details
- **Lecture 9:** Introduction to hay conditioning, its importance, methods of hay conditioning 2 and calculation of moisture content of hay
- **Lecture 10:** Introduction to threshing systems- manual and mechanical systems, Types of 2 threshing drums and their applications
- **Lecture 11:** Introduction to various types of threshers- tangential and axial, constructional 1 details and cleaning systems, Study of factors affecting thresher performance
- **Lecture 12:** Study of grain combines- combine terminology and features, classification of 2 grain combines, study of material flow in combines
- **Lecture 13:** Computation of combine losses, Study of combine troubles and 2 troubleshooting
- **Lecture 14:** Study of chaff cutters- working principle, constructional features and capacity 1 calculations
- **Lecture 15:** Study of straw combines- working principle and constructional details
- **Lecture 16:** Study of root crop diggers- principles of operation, functional components, 2 blade adjustment and approach angle and calculation of material handled
- **Lecture 17:** Study of cotton harvesting- cotton harvesting mechanisms, cotton pickers and 1 strippers
- **Lecture 18:** Study of maize harvesting combines
- **Lecture 19:** Introduction to vegetables and fruit harvesting equipment and tools
- **Lecture 20:** Study of testing and evaluation of intercultural, plant protection and harvesting 1 machinery and their test codes
