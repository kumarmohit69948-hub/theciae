# REE4.7 — Renewable Energy Sources

- **Lecture 1:** Different sources of renewable energy- concepts and limitations of Lectures different renewable energy sources (RES) as solar

- **Lecture 15:** Generation of power from biogas; Design & use of different 2 commercial biogas plants Power generation from urban, municipal and industrial waste

- **Lecture 17:** Power generation from biomass (gasification & Dendro- thermal);Mini 2 and micro hydel plants; Fuel cells and its associated parameters
