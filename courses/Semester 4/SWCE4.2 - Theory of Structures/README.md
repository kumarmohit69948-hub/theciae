# SWCE4.2 — Theory of Structures

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Types of Load and use of BIS code
- **Lecture 2:** Design of steel structures- specifications, use of IS code (IS 800-2007) and steel 3 table
- **Lecture 3:** Design of steel sections under tension
- **Lecture 4:** Design of steel sections under compression
- **Lecture 5:** Design of steel sections under bending
- **Lecture 6:** Use of any one design software such as Staad Pro, Etabs, etc. for design of roof 4 truss
- **Lecture 7:** Design of RCC structures- specifications, use of IS code (IS 456-2000)
- **Lecture 8:** Analysis and design of singly and doubly reinforced sections
- **Lecture 9:** Design of beams
- **Lecture 10:** Design of one way and two way slabs
- **Lecture 11:** Columns and foundations
- **Lecture 12:** Design considerations for retaining walls and silos
- **Lecture 13:** Use of design software for simple RCC structures
