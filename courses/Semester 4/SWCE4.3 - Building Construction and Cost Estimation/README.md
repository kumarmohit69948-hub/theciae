# SWCE4.3 — Building Construction and Cost Estimation

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Building materials: description of important building materials
- **Lecture 2:** Different stones; formation of stones
- **Lecture 3:** Types of stones & Quarrying process
- **Lecture 4:** Stone products and uses
- **Lecture 5:** Bricks, types, preparation
- **Lecture 6:** Burning of bricks, properties and uses
- **Lecture 7:** Types of tiles
- **Lecture 8:** Classification of Lime and its properties with uses
- **Lecture 9:** Classification of Cement and its different uses with graes
- **Lecture 10:** Concrete: grades, preparation, mixing and laying of concrete
- **Lecture 11:** Use of sand with classification
- **Lecture 12:** Use of ferrous material, iron and steel products
- **Lecture 13:** Use of non-ferrous metals, glass, rubber, plastics, aluminum, copper, nickel
- **Lecture 14:** Timber and its uses, seasoning, defects, commercial form of timber
- **Lecture 15:** Miscellaneous building materials
- **Lecture 16:** Building construction: building components
- **Lecture 18:** Different types of floors
- **Lecture 19:** Plastering and pointing, damp proofing, Waterproofing washing, distempering 1 and painting
- **Lecture 20:** Steps for building construction
- **Lecture 21:** Needs of different agricultural buildings, types and uses
- **Lecture 22:** Types of roofs, slope and flat roof buildings
- **Lecture 23:** Estimating and costing: types of estimates, rough cost, detailed and 2 supplementary estimate
- **Lecture 24:** Preparation of cost estimate, cost analysis, schedule of rates, analysis of rates
- **Lecture 25:** Factors affecting building costs
- **Lecture 26:** Building codes, estate development
- **Lecture 27:** Cost economics: measurement and pricing
- **Lecture 28:** Economic methods for evaluation of buildings
- **Lecture 29:** Benefit cost calculation
- **Lecture 30:** Rate of return period (payback period)
