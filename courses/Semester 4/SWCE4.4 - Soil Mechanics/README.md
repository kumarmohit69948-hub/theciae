# SWCE4.4 — Soil Mechanics

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Brief information about syllabus, Introduction to soil mechanics, field 1 and scope of soil mechanics
- **Lecture 2:** Phase diagram, physical and index properties of soil
- **Lecture 3:** Particle size distribution, grain size distribution curve
- **Lecture 4:** Soil indices; plastic limit, liquid limit, shrinkage limit
- **Lecture 5:** Classification of soils
- **Lecture 6:** Effective and neutral stress
- **Lecture 7:** Boussinesq and Wester-guard's analysis; New-mark's influence chart, stress distribution and diagrams
- **Lecture 8:** Shear stress, Mohr's circle, direct shear stress, triaxial test and vane shear test; Mohr coulomb failure theory, effective stress principle, determination of shear parameters by direct shear test, triangle test & vane shear test. Numerical exercise based on various types of tests
- **Lecture 10:** Consolidation of soils, Terzaghi's theory of one dimensional 2 consolidation, spring analogy, Laboratory consolidation test, calculation of void ratio and coefficient of volume change
- **Lecture 11:** Taylor's and Casagrande's method Earth pressure: plastic equilibrium in 16 soils, active and passive states, Rankine's theory of earth pressure, active
