# SWCE4.5 — Soil and Water Conservation Engineering

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 4:** USLE. Rainfall erosivity-estimation by KE>25 and EI30 methods; Soil
- **Lecture 6:** Measurement of soil erosion- Runoff plots, soil samples Water erosion
- **Lecture 8:** Agronomical measures, contour farming, strip cropping, conservation
- **Lecture 10:** Engineering measures- bunds and terraces, bunds: contour and graded bunds 5 design and surplussing arrangements
- **Lecture 11:** Terraces: level and graded broad base terraces, bench terraces -planning
- **Lecture 13:** Gully and ravine reclamation- principles of gully control, vegetative 2 measures, temporary structures and diversion drains
- **Lecture 14:** Grassed waterways and design
