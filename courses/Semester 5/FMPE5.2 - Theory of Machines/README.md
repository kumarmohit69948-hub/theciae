# FMPE5.2 — Theory of Machines

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Simple mechanism: Elements, links, pairs, kinematics chain, and 2 mechanisms
- **Lecture 2:** Classification of pairs and mechanisms; lower and higher pairs
- **Lecture 3:** Four bar chain, slider crank chain and their inversions
- **Lecture 4:** Velocity mechanism: determination of velocity and acceleration using 4 graphical (instantaneous centres) method
- **Lecture 5:** Types of gears, law of gearing, velocity of sliding between two teeth in 2 mesh; Involute and cycloidal profile for gear teeth; Spur gear, nomenclature
- **Lecture 6:** Introduction to helical, spiral, bevel and worm gear
- **Lecture 7:** Simple, compound, reverted, and epicyclic trains; determining velocity ratio 3 by tabular method
- **Lecture 8:** Turning moment diagrams, coefficient of fluctuation of speed and energy, 2 weight of flywheel, flywheel applications
- **Lecture 9:** Belt drives: Types of drives, belt materials, length of belt, transmitted 3 power, velocity ratio, belt size for flat and V belts; effect of centrifugal tension, creep and slip on power transmission
- **Lecture 10:** Chain drives, classification of chain drive, terms used in chain drive
- **Lecture 11:** Types of friction, laws of dry friction; friction of pivots and collars; single 2 disc, multiple disc, and cone clutches, rolling friction
- **Lecture 13:** Static and dynamic balancing, balancing of rotating masses in one and 2 different planes
