# IDE5.5 — Irrigation and Drainage Engineering

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 2:** Major and medium irrigation schemes of India, purpose of irrigation
- **Lecture 6:** Measurement of irrigation water: weir, flumes and orifices and other 3 methods
- **Lecture 7:** Design and lining of irrigation field channels, on-farm structures for water conveyance, control & distribution
- **Lecture 8:** Underground pipe conveyance system: components and design
- **Lecture 9:** Land grading; Criteria for land levelling, land levelling design methods
- **Lecture 10:** Soil-water-plant relationship: soil properties influencing irrigation
- **Lecture 13:** Water requirement of crops: concept of evapotranspiration (ET)
- **Lecture 16:** Surface methods of water application: border, check basin and furrow
