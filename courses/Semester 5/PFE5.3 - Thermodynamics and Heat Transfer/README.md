# PFE5.3 — Thermodynamics and Heat Transfer

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Basic concepts and definitions of thermodynamics, statistical and classical 4 thermodynamics, microscopic and macroscopic point of view; Thermodynamic systems-thermodynamic equilibrium
- **Lecture 2:** Properties of systems; state, path, process, cycle ;point function, path function; 4 temperature and zeroth law of thermodynamics; pressure, specific volume, density, energy, work and heat
- **Lecture 3:** First law of thermodynamics- internal energy, law of conservation of energy, 4 first law of thermodynamics, application of first law to a process; energy-a property of system, perpetual motion machine of the first kind-PMM1
- **Lecture 4:** Characteristic equation of state, specific heats
- **Lecture 6:** Second law of thermodynamics ,Limitations of first law of thermodynamics 4 and introduction to second law, statements of second law of thermodynamics
- **Lecture 7:** Perpetual motion machine of the second kind-PMM2; Clausius inequality
- **Lecture 8:** Concept, modes of heat transfer, thermal conductivity of materials, 3 measurement, general differential equation of conduction
- **Lecture 9:** One dimensional steady state conduction through plane and composite walls, 5 tubes and spheres without heat generation, electrical analogy, insulation materials and fins; Free and forced convection
- **Lecture 10:** Newton's law of cooling, heat transfer coefficient in convection, non- 4 dimensional numbers; equation of laminar boundary layer on flat plate and in a tube, laminar forced convection on a flat plate and tube, combined free and forced convection
- **Lecture 11:** Thermal radiation, black body radiation, Stefan-Boltzmann law, black body 3 emissive power, emissivity, absorptivity, reflectivity and transmissivity
- **Lecture 12:** Heat transfer analysis involving conduction, convection and radiation
- **Lecture 13:** Types of heat exchangers; fouling, log mean temperature difference ,heat 3 exchanger performance, transfer units; Heat exchanger analysis restricted to parallel and counter flow heat exchangers
- **Lecture 14:** Introduction to mass transfer, analogy between heat and mass transfer, Fick's 4 law of diffusion
