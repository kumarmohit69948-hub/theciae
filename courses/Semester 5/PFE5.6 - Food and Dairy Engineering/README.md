# PFE5.6 — Food and Dairy Engineering

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Introduction to different unit operations in food processing
- **Lecture 2:** Process flow charts for preparation of various food products
- **Lecture 3:** Mass and energy balance
- **Lecture 4:** Dehydration of foods; dryers for solid foods, construction and operation 3 of direct and indirect type solar dryers, tray dryer, tunnel dryer, vacuum dryer, microwave dryer, freeze dryer, etc
- **Lecture 5:** Dryers for liquid foods, construction and operation of drum dryer, spray 3 dryer and vacuum band dryer
- **Lecture 6:** Evaporation of food products: principle, different types of evaporators, 3 factors affecting steam economy, multiple effect evaporation, vapour recompression
- **Lecture 7:** Thermal processing: thermobacteriology, D value, Z value, reaction 3 quotient, process time, different types of retorts and continuous sterilizers, canning process, aseptic processing
- **Lecture 8:** Principles and applications of different non-thermal processing methods 3 as vacuum processing, high pressure processing, PEF processing
- **Lecture 9:** Principles and applications of novel heating methods, viz. ohmic, 2 infrared and dielectric heating
- **Lecture 10:** Mixing: Theory of mixing of solids and pastes, mixing index, mixers for 4 solids, liquid foods and pastes, viz. tumbling mixer, screw mixer, ribbon mixer, liquid mixers, sigma-blade mixer and anchor and gate agitator
- **Lecture 11:** Separation processes: principle and equipment for sedimentation of 3 solids in liquid and solids in air; Principle and operation of tubular bowl centrifuge and disc bowl centrifuge
- **Lecture 12:** Filtration: principle, construction and working principles of different 3 types of filters as plate and frame filter press, shell and leaf filter, centrifugal filter, rotary drum filter, continuous belt filter
- **Lecture 13:** Membrane separation: principle, characteristics and applications of 2 reverse osmosis, nanofiltration, ultra-filtration and macro-filtration; membrane modules
- **Lecture 14:** Extrusion cooking: principle, factors affecting extrusion cooking, single 2 and twin screw extruders
- **Lecture 15:** Unit operations in milk processing: Engineering, thermal and chemical 4 properties of milk and milk products; Principles and equipment related
- **Lecture 17:** Filling and packaging: Selection of different types of packaging
