# IDE6.2 — Groundwater, Wells and Pumps

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 3:** Groundwater hydrology and hydrologic cycle, groundwater resources
- **Lecture 5:** Occurrence and movement of ground water, aquifer and its types
- **Lecture 6:** Aquifer properties, groundwater flow direction in relation to 3 groundwater contours
- **Lecture 7:** Classification of wells, fully penetrating tube wells and open wells
- **Lecture 9:** Darcy's law, determination of hydraulic conductivity by laboratory and 3 field method
- **Lecture 10:** Groundwater hydraulics- Dupit's assumptions and Dupit's method
- **Lecture 11:** Thiem's method; Well interference; determination of aquifer
- **Lecture 13:** Design of tube well and gravel pack, sanitary protection of tube wells
- **Lecture 14:** Groundwater exploration techniques
- **Lecture 16:** Development of tube well; Basin wise ground water development, safe 3 yield, factors governing safe yield, computation of safe yield by Hill's 3 method, conjunctive use of ground water
