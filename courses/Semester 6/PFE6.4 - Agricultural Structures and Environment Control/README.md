# PFE6.4 — Agricultural Structures and Environment Control

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Farm and farmstead, farmstead planning and lay out
- **Lecture 2:** Environmental control- scope, importance and need
- **Lecture 3:** Physiological reaction of livestock
- **Lecture 4:** Environmental control, systems and design
- **Lecture 5:** Control of temperature, humidity and air ventilation
- **Lecture 10:** Rural housing and development; Farm roads- types of roads in the farm, 2 construction methods, repair and maintenance of rural roads
- **Lecture 12:** Site selection and orientation of buildings for sanitation. Sewage system 4 and design, maintenance, septic tank for small family
- **Lecture 13:** Rural electrification- estimate of domestic power requirement, sources 3 of power supply, electrification for rural housing
