# PFE6.6 — Refrigeration and Air-conditioning

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Definition of pure substance, phases of a pure substance, phase change process of a pure substances; compressed liquid & saturated liquid, saturated vapour and superheated vapour, saturated temperature & saturated pressure
- **Lecture 2:** Latent heat: latent heat of fusion, latent heat of vaporization; liquid vapour saturation curve; property diagram for phase change process, T-V diagram, P-
- **Lecture 3:** Property tables, state- liquid & vapour states, saturated liquid-vapour mixture, 1 superheated vapour, compressed liquid
- **Lecture 4:** Principles of refrigeration, units, terminology, production of low temperatures
- **Lecture 5:** Air refrigerators working on reverse Carnot cycle and Bell Coleman cycle
- **Lecture 6:** Vapour refrigeration-mechanism, P-V, T- S,P-h diagrams,vapour 4 compression cycles, dry and wet compression, super cooling and sub cooling
- **Lecture 7:** Vapour absorption refrigeration system
- **Lecture 8:** Common refrigerants and their properties
- **Lecture 9:** Thermodynamic properties of moist air, perfect gas relationship for 2 approximate calculation, adiabatic saturation process, wet bulb temperature and its measurement, psychometric chart and its use
- **Lecture 10:** Elementary psychometric processes
- **Lecture 11:** Air conditioning: principles, type and functions of air conditioning, 1 physiological principles in air conditioning
- **Lecture 12:** Air distribution, factors considered for designing an air-conditioning system
- **Lecture 13:** Types of air conditioners and their applications
- **Lecture 14:** Cold storage plants; calculation of refrigeration load and cold storage design 3 considerations
