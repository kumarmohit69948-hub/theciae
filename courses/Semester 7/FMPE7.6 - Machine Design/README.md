# FMPE7.6 — Machine Design

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Phases of design, design considerations
- **Lecture 2:** Common engineering materials and their mechanical properties
- **Lecture 3:** Types of loads and stresses
- **Lecture 4:** Theories of failure, factor of safety, selection of allowable stress
- **Lecture 6:** Design of shafts under torsion and combined bending and torsion
- **Lecture 7:** Design of keys; Design of muff, sleeve, and rigid flange couplings
- **Lecture 8:** Cotter joints, design of socket and spigot cotter joint; knuckle joint
- **Lecture 9:** Design of welded subjected to static loads
- **Lecture 10:** Design of helical and leaf springs
- **Lecture 11:** Design of threaded fasteners subjected to direct static loads, bolted joints 3 loaded in shear and bolted joints subjected to eccentric loading
- **Lecture 12:** Design of flat belt drives and pulleys
- **Lecture 13:** Design of V-belt drives and pulleys
- **Lecture 14:** Design of gears
