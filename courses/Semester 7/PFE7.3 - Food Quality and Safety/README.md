# PFE7.3 — Food Quality and Safety

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Basics of food quality, safety and food analysis; Concept, objectives and need 3 of food quality; definition, objective measurement of quality and quality and safety indices
- **Lecture 2:** Quality control, quality control tools, statistical quality control; Sampling 4 (Chemical and Microbiological): purpose, sampling techniques, sampling procedures for liquid, powdered and granular materials
- **Lecture 3:** Instrumental method for testing food quality, measurement of colour, flavour, consistency, viscosity, texture and their relationship with food quality and 3 composition
- **Lecture 4:** Non-destructive methods for evaluation of food quality. NIR, FTIR and 4 chemometrics theory and application in food quality prediction
- **Lecture 5:** Theory and application of X-ray, CT, MRI, Ultrasound for internal quality inspection of fruits and vegetables. Sorting grading using external image analysis, internal biochemical analysis using spectroscopy
- **Lecture 6:** Sensory evaluation methods, panel selection methods, Interpretation of sensory results
- **Lecture 7:** Food hazards and food safety, Food borne infections, contaminants (physical, chemical, biological), adulteration, food safety strategies- Food Safety
- **Lecture 8:** Hazards and HACCP, Sanitation in food industry (SSOP); Food Laws and
- **Lecture 9:** Food Recall, Traceability; Bio safety and Bioterrorism; Sanitation in food industry
