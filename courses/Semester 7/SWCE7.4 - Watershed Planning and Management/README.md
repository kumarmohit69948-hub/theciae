- **Lecture 11:** Use of remote sensing thematic maps and GIS in watershed planning
- **Lecture 12:** Watershed programme- execution, follow-up practices, maintenance, 2 monitoring and evaluation
- **Lecture 13:** Planning and formulation of project proposal for watershed management 3 programme including costbenefit analysis
- **Lecture 14:** Financial management and accounting procedure
