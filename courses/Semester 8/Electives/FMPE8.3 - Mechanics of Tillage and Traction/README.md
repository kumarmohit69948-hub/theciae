# FMPE8.3 — Mechanics of Tillage and Traction

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Introduction to mechanics of tillage tools
- **Lecture 2:** Engineering properties of soil
- **Lecture 3:** Principles and concepts, stress strain relationship
- **Lecture 4:** Design of tillage tools, principles of soil cutting, design equation, force analysis
- **Lecture 5:** Application of dimensional analysis in soil dynamics and traction prediction 3 equation
- **Lecture 6:** Introduction to traction and mechanics
- **Lecture 7:** Off-road traction and mobility, traction model, traction improvement, tyres- 3 functions, size, lug geometry and their effects
- **Lecture 8:** Tyre selection and testing
- **Lecture 9:** Soil compaction and plant growth and variability
- **Lecture 10:** Application of GIS in soil dynamics
