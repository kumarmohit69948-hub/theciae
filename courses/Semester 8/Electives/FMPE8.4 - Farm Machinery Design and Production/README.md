# FMPE8.4 — Farm Machinery Design and Production

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Introduction to design parameters of agricultural machines and design 2 procedure
- **Lecture 2:** Characteristics of farm machinery design, research and development aspects 3 of farm machinery
- **Lecture 3:** Introduction to safety in power transmission
- **Lecture 4:** Design of standard power transmission components used in agricultural 2 machines: mechanical and hydraulic units
- **Lecture 5:** Application of design principles to the systems of selected farm machines such 5 as design of disc plough, cultivator, seed drill, reaper, thresher and digger
- **Lecture 6:** Critical appraisal in production of agricultural machinery
- **Lecture 7:** Advances in material used for agricultural machinery
- **Lecture 8:** Cutting tools including CNC tools and finishing tools
- **Lecture 9:** Heat treatment of steels including pack carburizing, shot pining process, etc., 3 limits, fits and tolerances, jigs and fixtures
- **Lecture 10:** Industrial lay-out planning
- **Lecture 12:** Economics of process selection
- **Lecture 13:** Familiarization with project report
