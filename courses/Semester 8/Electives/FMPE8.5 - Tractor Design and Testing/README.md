# FMPE8.5 — Tractor Design and Testing

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Procedure for design and development of agricultural tractor
- **Lecture 2:** Study of parameters for balanced design of tractor for stability and weight 3 distribution
- **Lecture 3:** Traction theory
- **Lecture 4:** Hydraulic lift and hitch system design
- **Lecture 5:** Design of mechanical power transmission in agricultural tractors: single disc, 4 multi disc and cone clutches
- **Lecture 6:** Rolling friction and anti-friction bearings
- **Lecture 7:** Study of special design features of tractor engines and their selection, viz. 4 cylinder, piston, piston pin, crankshaft, etc
- **Lecture 8:** Design of seat and controls of an agricultural tractor
- **Lecture 9:** Tractor Testing
