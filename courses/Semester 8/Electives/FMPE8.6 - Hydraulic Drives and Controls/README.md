# FMPE8.6 — Hydraulic Drives and Controls

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Basics of hydraulics: Pascal's law, flow, energy, work, and power
- **Lecture 2:** Hydraulic systems, colour coding, reservoirs
- **Lecture 3:** Strainers and filters, filtering material and elements, accumulators
- **Lecture 4:** Pressure gauges and volume meters
- **Lecture 5:** Hydraulic circuit, fittings and connectors
- **Lecture 6:** Pumps and its classifications, operation, performance, displacement
- **Lecture 8:** Hydraulic motors
- **Lecture 9:** Valves, pressure-control valves, directional- control valves, flow-control 4 valves, valve installation, valve failures and remedies, valve assembly, troubleshooting of valves
- **Lecture 10:** Hydraulic circuit diagrams
- **Lecture 11:** USA Standards Institute (USASI) symbols
- **Lecture 12:** Tractor hydraulics, nudging system, ADDC
- **Lecture 13:** Application of hydraulics and pneumatics drives in agricultural systems
