# FMPE8.7 — Human Engineering and Safety

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Human factors in system development- concept of systems
- **Lecture 2:** Basic processes in system development, performance reliability, human 3 performance
- **Lecture 3:** Information input process, visual displays, major types and use of displays, 3 auditory displays; Speech communications
- **Lecture 4:** Biomechanics of motion, types of movements, range of movements
- **Lecture 5:** Strength and endurance, speed and accuracy, human control of systems
- **Lecture 6:** Human motor activities, controls, tools and related devices
- **Lecture 7:** Atmospheric conditions, thermo- regulation in human, thermal comfort
- **Lecture 8:** Environmental factors, air pollution
- **Lecture 9:** Dangerous machine (Regulation) act, rehabilitation and compensation to 3 accident victims
- **Lecture 10:** Safety gadgets for spraying, threshing, chaff cutting and tractor and trailer 5 operation, etc
