# FMPE8.8 — Precision Agriculture and System Management

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Precision agriculture- need and functional requirements
- **Lecture 2:** Familiarization with issues relating to natural resources
- **Lecture 3:** Equipment for precision agriculture including sowing and planting machines
- **Lecture 4:** Study of power sprayers
- **Lecture 5:** Study of land clearing machines
- **Lecture 6:** Study of laser guided land levelers
- **Lecture 7:** Study of straw-chopper, straw-balers, grain combines, etc
- **Lecture 8:** Introduction to GIS based precision agriculture and its applications
- **Lecture 9:** Introduction to sensors and application of sensors for data generation
- **Lecture 10:** Database management; System concept, system approach in farm machinery 3 management
- **Lecture 11:** Problems on machinery selection
- **Lecture 12:** Maintenance and scheduling of operations
- **Lecture 13:** Application of PERT and CPM in machinery system management
