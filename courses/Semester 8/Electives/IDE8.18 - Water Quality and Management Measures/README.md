# IDE8.18 — Water Quality and Management Measures

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Natural factors affecting quality of surface water and groundwater
- **Lecture 2:** Sources and pollution of groundwater
- **Lecture 3:** Water quality objectives in relation to domestic, industrial and agricultural 4 activities
- **Lecture 4:** Drinking water quality standards
- **Lecture 5:** Irrigation water quality classification as per USSL and AICRP criteria
- **Lecture 6:** Point and non-point water pollution sources
- **Lecture 8:** Water contamination related to agricultural chemicals, food industry, 2 hydrocarbon and synthetic organic compounds
- **Lecture 9:** Arsenic and fluoride contamination in groundwater and remedial measures
