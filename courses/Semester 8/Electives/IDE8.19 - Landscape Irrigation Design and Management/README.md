# IDE8.19 — Landscape Irrigation Design and Management

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 5:** Segments of landscape irrigation systems, main components of modern 3 landscape irrigation systems and their selection criteria
- **Lecture 6:** Types of pipes, pressure ratings, sizing and selection criteria
- **Lecture 7:** Automation system for landscape irrigation- main components, types of 4 controllers and their application
- **Lecture 8:** Use of sensors for irrigation automation and use of IOT in landscape 3 irrigation
- **Lecture 9:** Use of AutoCAD in irrigation design
- **Lecture 10:** Design of modern landscape irrigation systems, operation and maintenance 2 of landscape irrigation systems
