# PFE8.23 — Development of Processed Food Products

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Process of new product development; Process flow chart with mass and 4 energy balance
- **Lecture 2:** Unit operations and equipment for processing; Technologies for value 6 addition of cereals, pulses and oil seeds- milled, puffed, flaked, roasted and malted products, bakery products, snack food, extruded products
- **Lecture 3:** Technologies for value added products from fruits, vegetables and spices as 6 canned foods, frozen foods, dried foods, fried foods, fruit juices, sauce, sugar based confectionery, candy, fermented products, spice extract
- **Lecture 5:** Technologies for value addition of forest produce as mahua and tamarind
- **Lecture 6:** Technology for processing of animal produce, viz. meat, poultry, fish, egg 4 products
- **Lecture 7:** Technologies for preparation of health foods, nutraceuticals and functional 4 food; Organic food processing
