# PFE8.24 — Food Packaging Technology

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 2:** General principles of control of the spoilage agents
- **Lecture 3:** Difference between food infection, food intoxication and allergy
- **Lecture 4:** Packaging of foods, requirement, importance and scope, 2 environmental considerations
- **Lecture 6:** Metal cans- manufacture of two pieces and three piece cans
- **Lecture 7:** Plastic packaging- different types of polymers and lamination used in 1 food packaging and their barrier properties
- **Lecture 9:** Glass containers- types of glass used in food packaging, manufacture 2 of glass and glass containers, closures for glass containers
- **Lecture 11:** Relative advantages and disadvantages of different packaging 2 materials and Effect of these materials on packed commodities
- **Lecture 12:** Nutritional labelling on packages
- **Lecture 13:** CAS and MAP, shrink and cling packaging, vacuum and gas 2 packaging; Active packaging, Smart packaging
- **Lecture 14:** Packaging requirement for raw and processed foods and selection of 2 packaging materials
- **Lecture 15:** Disposal and recycle of packaging waste
- **Lecture 16:** Package testing- testing methods for flexible materials, rigid materials 1 and semi rigid materials
- **Lecture 18:** Plastic film and laminates (thickness, tensile strength, gloss, haze, 1 burning test to identify polymer, etc.)
- **Lecture 19:** Aluminium foil (thickness, pin holes, etc.)
- **Lecture 20:** Glass containers (visual defects, colour, dimensions, impact strength, 1 etc.)
- **Lecture 21:** Metal containers (pressure test, product compatibility, etc.)
