# PFE8.25 — Food Plant and Equipment Design

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 2:** Food plant location, selection criteria for plant location; Selection 2 of processes and plant capacity; Requirements of plant building and its components, flow diagrams
- **Lecture 4:** Application of design engineering for processing equipment
- **Lecture 5:** Design codes
- **Lecture 6:** Pressure vessel design
- **Lecture 7:** Design of cleaners
- **Lecture 8:** Design of tubular heat exchanger
- **Lecture 9:** Design of shell and tube heat exchanger
- **Lecture 10:** Design of plate heat exchanger
- **Lecture 11:** Design of belt conveyer
- **Lecture 12:** Design of screw conveyer
- **Lecture 13:** Design of bucket conveyer
- **Lecture 14:** Design of grain dryers
- **Lecture 15:** Design of milling equipment
- **Lecture 16:** Optimization of design with respect to process efficiency, energy 2 and cost
- **Lecture 17:** Computer Aided Design
