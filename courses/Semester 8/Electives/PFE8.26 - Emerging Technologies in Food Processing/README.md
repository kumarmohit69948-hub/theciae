# PFE8.26 — Emerging Technologies in Food Processing

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Introduction, different emerging technologies and their scope and 4 applications
- **Lecture 2:** Principle, equipment and applications of ohmic heating, infrared heating, 6 dielectric heating, microwave heating systems
- **Lecture 3:** Principle, equipment and applications of radio frequency heating 8 equipment, combined microwave vacuum drying new hybrid drying technologies
- **Lecture 4:** Principles and equipment for vacuum processing, High pressure processing
- **Lecture 5:** Principles and equipment for Ultrasonication, Gamma irradiation/ ionising 6 radiation, Ultraviolet radiation processing
- **Lecture 6:** Pulsed X-ray processing, Pulsed light processing, Cold plasma Processing
- **Lecture 7:** Ozone treatment, Electron beam processing
- **Lecture 8:** Static and oscillating magnetic fields, Dense phase carbon dioxide, High 4 voltage arc discharge
- **Lecture 9:** Nanomaterial utilisation in food processing, manufacture of nanomaterials, 5 applications
