# PFE8.28 — Food Business Management and Entrepreneurship Development

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Introduction and definitions related with project management and 2 entrepreneurship
- **Lecture 2:** Fundamentals of project management and entrepreneurship development
- **Lecture 3:** Project formulation: market survey techniques, project identification, 5 project selection, project proposal, work breakdown structure
- **Lecture 4:** Network scheduling: activity, networks, use of CPM, PERT in project 4 scheduling
- **Lecture 5:** Resource planning, resource allocation, project scheduling with limited 3 resources
- **Lecture 6:** Estimation of project costs, earned value analysis, project techno-economic 4 viability, break-even analysis
- **Lecture 8:** Government policies for promotion of entrepreneurship in food processing
- **Lecture 9:** Launching and organizing an enterprise, enterprise selection, market 6 assessment, feasibility study, SWOT analysis
- **Lecture 10:** Resource mobilization
- **Lecture 11:** Financial institution in promoting entrepreneurship
- **Lecture 12:** Supply chain management
- **Lecture 13:** Case study of a food business
