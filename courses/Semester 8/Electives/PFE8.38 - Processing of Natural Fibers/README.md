## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Concept of spinning: Hand spinning system; Charkha spinning system
- **Lecture 2:** Concept of Yarn Manufacture: cotton system, woollen system, worsted 2 system, jute system, flax (wet) system: Blow room, Carding (Flat type and roller-clearer), Drawing machine, roving machine
- **Lecture 3:** Ring spinning, Rotor spinning; Modern developments in spinning; Principle 3 of ring doubler and two-for-one twister; Fiber packing density of yarn; Yarn twist and its relation to yarn properties. Stress-strain relation, Mass irregularity
- **Lecture 4:** Preparatory weaving: Winding, Warping, Sizing, beaming, drawing and 2 denting; Weaving: Concept of weaving, Handloom, Primary and secondary motions of loom
- **Lecture 5:** Shedding, picking, Beatup, Loom timing, Take-up and Let-off motions
- **Lecture 6:** Basic woven fabric structure and design; Knitting: Concept of knitting
- **Lecture 7:** Basic Yarn testing: Count, Twist, tensile strength, CSP, Hairiness, Fabric 4 testing: GSM, EPI, PPI, Tensile strength, tearing strength, Bursting strength
- **Lecture 8:** Chemical composition of plant and animal fibers - Natural and added 2 impurities; Pre-treatment of natural fiber for surface cleaning/ removing impurities ­ Desizing
- **Lecture 9:** Scouring; Degumming; Bleaching- reductive, oxidative; combined scouring 3 and bleaching; Woollenization; Mercerization; fluorescent brightening agents; de-pigmentation; cottonisation; Carbonization, Felting of wool
- **Lecture 10:** Value-addition of natural fiber by colouration- Introduction to dyes and 2 pigments; Classification of dyes based on the source and application
- **Lecture 11:** The colouration of natural fibers- Direct, Acid, basic, Reactive, Vat
- **Lecture 12:** Dyeing machine; Dyeing of different textile forms-Hank yarn, Cone, Loose
