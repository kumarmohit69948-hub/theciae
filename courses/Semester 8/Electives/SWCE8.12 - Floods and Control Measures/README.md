# SWCE8.12 — Floods and Control Measures

B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering (ICAR 6th Deans' Committee syllabus).

## Lecture plan

- **Lecture 1:** Floods- causes of occurrence, flood classification- probable maximum 2 flood, standard project flood, design flood
- **Lecture 2:** Flood estimation- methods of estimation; Estimation of flood peak- 4 rational method, empirical methods, unit hydrograph method
- **Lecture 3:** Statistics in hydrology, flood frequency methods- lognormal
- **Lecture 5:** Flood routing- channel routing, Muskingum method, reservoir 3 routing, modified Pul's method
- **Lecture 6:** Flood control- history of flood control
- **Lecture 7:** Structural and non-structural measures of flood control, storage and 3 detention reservoirs, levees, channel improvement
- **Lecture 8:** Gully erosion and its control structures- design and implementation
- **Lecture 9:** Earthen embankments- functions, classification, hydraulic fill and 4 rolled fill dams, homogeneous, zoned and diaphragm type, foundation requirements, grouting, seepage through dams, flow net and its properties, seepage pressure, seepage line in composite earth embankments, drainage filters, piping and its causes
- **Lecture 10:** Design and construction of earthen dam, stability of earthen 3 embankments against failure by tension, overturning, sliding, etc
- **Lecture 11:** Stability of slopes- analysis of failure by different methods; Planning 3 of flood control projects and their economics
